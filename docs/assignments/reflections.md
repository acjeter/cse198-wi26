# Post-Lesson Reflections

You are required to write **two (2)** reflection papers after attending designated CSE 198 lessons.

## Requirements
- **Count:** 2 Total.
- **Length:** 1â€“2 pages each (double spaced, 12pt font, pick any serif font).
- **Topic:** Reflect on the lesson delivery, student engagement, and your role. You might choose to talk about what went well, what could be improved, or a mix of both.

## Content Guidelines
Your reflection must include:
1. **Observation:** What happened during the lesson? How did students react to the material?
2. **Analysis:** What went well? What could be improved? Were there topics that students didn't quite understand? If so, why do you think that was?
3. **Pedagogy Research:** Summarize a relevant piece of pedagogy literature (paper, article, or theory) and apply it to your observation. (You may use the same pedagogy for both reflecitons.)
   - *Example:* "I observed students struggling with variable scoping. According to [Author]'s theory on mental models..."
   - *Example:* "I researched the teachng pedagogy of Active Questioning, and I applied it to my observation of a lesson on internet networking by..."
