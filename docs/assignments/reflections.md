# Post-Lesson Reflections

You are required to write **two (2)** reflection papers after attending designated CSE 198 lessons.

## Requirements
- **Count:** 2 Total.
- **Length:** 1â€“2 pages each (single spaced, standard font).
- **Topic:** Reflect on the lesson delivery, student engagement, and your role.

## Content Guidelines
Your reflection must include:
1. **Observation:** What happened during the lesson? How did students react?
2. **Analysis:** What went well? What could be improved?
3. **Pedagogy Research:** Summarize a relevant piece of pedagogy literature (paper, article, or theory) and apply it to your observation.
   - *Example:* "I observed students struggling with variable scoping. According to [Author]'s theory on mental models..."

## Submission
Submit via the link on the [Submissions](../submissions.html) page by the end of the quarter.
