# Final Presentation & Report

## Final Presentation
- **When:** Week 10 Class Meeting.
- **Format:** Individual presentation.
- **Duration:** 2–3 minutes.
- **Content:**
  - Overview of lessons developed/taught.
  - Key challenges and successes.
  - Summary of your reflections and feedback.

## Final Report
A written summary of your outreach work. Can be replaced by a **Technical Report** in special cases.

### Optional: Technical Report
- **Eligibility:** Students petitioning for specific credit (usually after 2 quarters).
- **Length:** 2–4 pages.
- **Content:** Deep dive into the technical content of the lesson or the pedagogical engineering.

## Submission
Slides and reports are due by the start of the Week 10 meeting. See [Submissions](../submissions.html).
