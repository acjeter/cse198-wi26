# Final Presentation & Report

## Final Presentation
- **When:** Week 10 Class Meeting.
- **Format:** Individual presentation.
- **Duration:** 2–3 minutes.
- **Content:**
  - Overview of lessons developed/taught.
  - Key challenges and successes.
  - Summary of your reflections and feedback.

## Final Report
A written summary of your outreach work. Completed when extenuating circumstances prevent the presentation of a Final Presentation. Can further be replaced by a **Technical Report** in special cases.

{: .warning-title}
> **Note**
> 
> Not everyone needs to complete a final report. Report completion is only for those seeking Technical Elective Credit. This is detailed further in the [Policies](../policies.html) page.

### Petitioning for Technical Credit: Technical Report
- **Eligibility:** Students petitioning for specific credit (usually after 2 quarters). Visit the [Policies](../policies.html) page to determine your need to complete a Technical Report.
- **Length:** 2–4 pages.
- **Content:** Deep dive into the technical content of the lesson or the pedagogical engineering.
- **Requirements:** [Syllabus](../syllabus.html) and [Policies](../policies.html).

## Submission
Slides and reports are due by the start of the Week 10 meeting. See [Submissions](../submissions.html).
