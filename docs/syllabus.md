## Course Overview

**CSE 198: HKN Outreach** is a 2-credit Directed Group Study course where students collaborate across MAE, CSE, and ECE disciplines to deliver interactive lessons to K-12 students.

### Goals
- Design and teach technical lessons.
- Build confidence in public speaking and communication.
- Collaborate in a multidisciplinary team.
- Serve the community by engaging with socioeconomically disadvantaged students.

### Logistics
- **Units:** 2 Credits (P/NP)
- **Meetings:** Weekly 1-hour meetings.
- **Optional:** Presentation skills sessions (Early quarter).

---

## Requirements & Grading

This course is graded **Pass/No Pass**. To earn a Pass, you must fulfill **ALL** of the following requirements:

### 1. Attendance & Participation
- Attend at least **80%** of weekly meetings.
- Actively contribute to group lesson work.

### 2. Outreach Minimums
You must attend at least **5 lessons/events total** during the quarter.
- At least **2** must be designated **CSE 198 lessons**.
- MAE/ECE lessons can count toward the remaining total.
- Lessons are assigned points in the HKN Portal.

### 3. Deliverables
- **Mandated Reporter Training:** Complete by Week 4.
- **Content Quizzes:** Pass quiz 1 day before any CSE lesson you attend.
- **Feedback Forms:** Submit within 24 hours of every lesson attended.
- **Reflections:** Write **2 total** (1-2 pages each) on CSE lessons attended, including pedagogy research.
- **Final Presentation:** Individual 2-3 minute presentation in Week 10.
- **Final Report:** (Or optional Technical Report for petitions).

> [!WARNING]
> Failure to meet the outreach minimums or submit required reflections/training will result in a No Pass.

## Policies

### Lesson Workflow
1. **Design:** Groups design lessons.
2. **Finalize:** Lesson + Quiz finalized **2 days prior** to event.
3. **Quiz:** Attendees must pass the validation quiz **1 day prior** to event to be assigned a ride.
4. **Teach:** Attend the event.
5. **Feedback:** Submit feedback form within **24 hours**.

### Mandated Reporter Training
Required by UC policy for working with minors.
- **Due:** Week 4.
- **Action:** Complete "CANRA" and "Abuse Risk Management for Volunteers" trainings.
- **Submission:** Email/Upload certificate (or screenshot of alternative course completion if access issues arise).
