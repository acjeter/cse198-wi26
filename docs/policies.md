# Policies & FAQ
{: .no_toc }

## Attendance
**Q: How many meetings can I miss?**

A: You must attend **80%** of the weekly meetings. If you have any conflicts, notify the CSE Outreach Chair (Andrew Jeter) immediately.

## Outreach Points
**Q: Do MAE/ECE lessons count?**

A: Yes, they count towards your **5 total events**. However, **2** of the 5 must be specific **CSE 198 lessons**.

## Technical Elective Credit
**Q: Do I need to submit a Technical Report?**

A: Only students seeking a form of CSE/ECE Technical Elective Credit or Experiential Learning Credit need to submit a Technical Report. Otherwise, you'll simply complete the final presentation for your end of quarter 'report'.

{: .note }
> 1. For Seventh College students, taking this course **one** time will fulfill your 2-credit "High Impact GE" requirement.
> 2. For Sixth College students, you will need to take this course **twice** to fulfill your 4-credit "Experiential Learning" requirement, and complete a Technical Report as part of your Sixth College petition. Please contact the CSE Outreach Chair (Andrew Jeter) for more information.

## Late Work
**Q: Can I submit reflections late?**

A: Reflections are accepted on a rolling basis, but we recommend submitting them within a week of the lesson while it's fresh. All work must be in by Week 10.

**Q: Can I take the content quiz late?**

A: **No.** The content quiz deadline (1 day prior) is strict because it affects ride planning. Please contact the CSE Outreach Chair (Andrew Jeter) if you have any extenuating circumstances.

## Communication
**Q: Where should I ask questions?**

A: Use **Discord**. It is the fastest way to reach staff and other students. Email is reserved for private matters.
