# Policies & FAQ
{: .no_toc }

## Attendance
**Q: How many meetings can I miss?**
A: You must attend **80%** of the weekly meetings. If you have a conflict, notify the staff immediately.

## Outreach Points
**Q: Do MAE/ECE lessons count?**
A: Yes, they count towards your **5 total events**. However, **2** of the 5 must be specific **CSE 198 lessons**.

## Late Work
**Q: Can I submit reflections late?**
A: Reflections are accepted on a rolling basis, but we recommend submitting them within a week of the lesson while it's fresh. All work must be in by Week 10.

**Q: Can I take the content quiz late?**
A: **No.** The content quiz deadline (1 day prior) is strict because it affects ride planning.

## Communication
**Q: Where should I ask questions?**
A: Use **Discord**. It is the fastest way to reach staff and other students. Email is reserved for private matters.
